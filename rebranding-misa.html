Objective

MISA is a Business club at Simon Fraser University (SFU) specializing in management information systems and with over 100+ members. My year long goal was to rebrand MISA into a professional club through MISA's website, promotional materials, sponsorship package, and events.

Team: Candy Leong, Kevin Lee, Esther Gutman, Louie Kwan, Jacky Yu, William Fong

Role: Marketing, Branding, Graphic Design

Tools: Wordpress, HTML/CSS, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Google Apps

Redesigned Website

The MISA website before my term was very outdated (sorry, no pictures were taken beforehand) and lacked constant upkeep or branding! I used a combination of html, css, and WordPress widgets to redesign the theme to fit MISA. Just a warning that the site may be changed since my term is over.

View the redesigned website

Promotional Materials

MISA had about 10 events throughout my term. For each event, I made a Facebook banner and an Instagram poster to share on social media platforms. Facebook views for the BC Aware: Protect Yourself event poster (right photo) reached over 1,000 likes in the span of 1 week compared to the usual 100 views for status messages.

Sponsorship Package

The sponsorship package was designed to connect with industry professionals that potentially could partner with MISA. A huge part in my job was to create content that was short, sweet, and got straight to the point.

View the sponsorship package

The Misa Mixer

The Misa Mixer is a semi-formal event connecting students to industry professionals in the Information Technology (IT) sector. I designed the logo and all promotional materials including: banners, small posters for sharing on social media, nametags, and booklets.

Reflection

Throughout my entire term as Vice President of Marketing, I gained valuable experience in marketing and branding. From designing promotional materials to website redesign, I expanded my skills in branding with every MISA event. I challenged myself to create different styles that fit wtih MISA.
