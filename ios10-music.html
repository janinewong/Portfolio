ios10 bleh

With the new iOS10 that recently came out, I had to say I was unimpressed at how many features Apple took out of the music player screen. So... I decided to make a revised version of the interface combining elements from iOS9 and iOS10, plus a bit of my own little flair.

iOS10 on the left. Redesigned on the right. Can you tell how big things are on the new iOS10?

So let's talk about how big everything is in iOS10 other than the actual artwork. The original iOS10 music design spots much larger font compared to its predecessors. It's ginormous. I mean hello... we're not blind. On another note, a lot of the buttons such as replay and shuffle aren't readily accessible anymore. You need to scroll down to see the buttons. Great, more things to do instead of having it right there.

In my redesign, I put back these features because they are buttons I normally use. I also added in a bar music graph to change up what was normally just a single line to keep track of the time left on the song. Well, I can only hope that in the next update Apple will at least decrease the font size. So much wasted space because of it.
