Electronic Arts

I turned wireframes into hi-fidelity mockups for both the mobile and web app on FUT (FIFA Ultimate Team) Companions. The focus was converting the Flash Web App into a responsive website.

My work included designing responsive screens, iconography, in-game art assets (FUT items), and marketing assets. I also found and suggested designs in the current app that would make it less frustrating for users. Designs were UX tested and feedback was incorporated before development.

Cross collaboration played a huge role in my work. I worked closely with developers and production on my team as well as designers and developers from console team to maintain a consistent and engaging experience across all platforms.

Role: Associate Interface Designer

Tools: Adobe Creative Suite, Invision, HTML Chrome Dev Tools
