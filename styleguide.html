Objective

MISA is a Business club at Simon Fraser University (SFU) specializing in management information systems and with over 100+ members. After being hired as the Vice President of Marketing, my year long goal was to rebrand MISA into a trustworthy and professional club through MISA's website and social media platforms.

Strategy

My first step for rebranding MISA was revamping their website. Then, I designed their sponsorship package which were handed out to companies in order to gain sponsors for the club. Afterwards, there were a series of events MISA held, which I designed the promotional materials for. This included their signature annual event, The Misa Mixer. Later in my term, I got the chance to organize an event partnering with ISACA called BC AWARE: Protect Yourself.

Revamped Website

The MISA website before my term was very outdated -- sorry, no pictures were taken beforehand, but trust me, it lacked constant upkeep and any sense of branding! It was run on WordPress, so I chose a theme called Adamos. I used a combination of html, css, and WordPress widgets to redesign the theme to fit MISA, while still keeping the old brand's colors and logo to ensure familiarity among its current members. P.S. Just a warning that since my term is over, the designs starting after May 2015 and before April 2014 are not mine!
