About

Knitstitch

Knit N Crochet 4 Better World needed a design intervention for their knitting club which only meets once a month in the Richmond Public Library. We gave them just that and more.

Client

Knit N Crochet 4 Better World

Contributors

Myself

Helen Bayogos

Role

Graphic Designer + Interface Prototyper

Timeline

Jan 2015 - Apr 2015

Objective

We saw the need to give a more social experience to the members of Knit N Crochet since they only met once a month. Our proposal to them: Knitstitch, an iPad application letting them share and start knitting projects together as long as they had wifi!

Strategy

Our first goal: getting to know Knit N Crochet like the back of our hand. We created cultural probes in their hand-crafted style to connect with them. We then explored different ways to solve a problem Knit N Crochet had through three concept posters and journey frameworks. Having narrowed down the concept to one, we implemented Knitstitch - a medium fidelity prototype on the iPad.
